About Us

Optimize trips to your nearby farmers market all while connecting with local producers you love. Where's My Veggie provides a connection between fresh produce lovers and fresh produce producers. Our website provides a simple, centralized way to track down what kinds of fresh produce you need, figure out who produces it, and where you can find them nearby. Developed by three junior coders with passions for fresh food and connecting with local producers, navigate to "Vendors" for a full list of local producers, and "Markets" for a full list of nearby markets. Inside each market, you'll find a complete list of producers who will be there on your next produce run.

Thanks for visiting!

Enjoy the opportunity to explore your nearby markets and vendors. Have any questions? Feel free to reach out!
